About Dr. Syed Vaqar Hussain

Dr. Syed Vaqar Hussain is a highly qualified neurosurgeon with 10 years of specialized experience in treating complex brain and spine conditions. He completed his MBBS and FCPS in Neurosurgery, demonstrating his commitment to excellence in neurosurgical practice.

Dr. Hussain specializes in brain and spine tumors, pediatric developmental anomalies, and traumatic brain and spinal injuries. His expertise encompasses advanced microsurgical and endoscopic techniques, with a strong focus on preserving neurological function and improving patient outcomes.
